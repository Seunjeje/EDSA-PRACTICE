AVANTGARDE SOCIAL SAFETY FOUNDATION

Avantgarde Social Safety Net Foundation is a subsidiary of Avantgarde Integrated Resources Limited located at Abuja, FCT. We have focused in assisting the widows, orphans, orphanages, less privileged, giving justice to the downtrodden and less Privileged. We give and build basic amenities to villages, hamlets that are far out of reach of civilization and government intervention. Our vision is to support humanities where help is needed most. Our motto is you don't have to be big before you can help, just a little can make a difference.
